# Lazynext Extension Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 28, 2026

## Overview
Lazynext provides a browser extension that allows users to capture active tab video and audio, which is then processed by our backend engine. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how it is used, and how it is secured.

## Data Collection
**1. Tab Capture Data**
The extension requests the `tabCapture` permission to record the visible content and audio of the active tab. This data is **only** captured when you explicitly click the "Start Recording" button in the extension popup.

**2. Temporary Storage**
Recorded chunks of media are buffered temporarily in memory before being securely transmitted to our API Gateway over HTTPS.

## Data Usage
The media captured is used strictly for the purpose of importing footage into your personal Lazynext timeline. It is not sold, shared with third-party advertisers, or used to train public AI models.

## Data Transmission & Security
All captured media is transmitted securely using TLS/SSL encryption to our cloud infrastructure and is stored in private encrypted storage.

## User Control
You can stop recording at any time by clicking "Stop Recording." The extension does not record in the background or monitor your browsing history (`tabs` permission is strictly used to identify the active tab for capture).
