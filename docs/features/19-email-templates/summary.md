# 📋 Summary — Email Templates

> **Feature**: #19 — Email Templates
> **Status**: ✅ Complete (Retroactive)
> **Approximate Date Range**: 2026-04 (designed) → shipped to `main`

## What Was Built

Four React-rendered transactional/digest email templates delivered via **Resend**: **Workspace Invite** (logo header, accept-invitation CTA, 7-day expiry), **Task Assignment** (compact header, task metadata grid, view-task CTA), **Weekly Digest** (Completed / In Progress / Blocked sections, decisions with quality scores, LazyMind observation callout), **Decision Digest** (gradient header, stats row, decision cards, tag-outcomes CTA). All templates render at max-width 600px for email-client compatibility and share a consistent header/footer with unsubscribe + privacy links.

## Key Decisions

- **Resend + React templates** — `@react-email` components compile to email-safe HTML; same source-of-truth as the app's design system (colors, type ramp).
- **Weekly Digest embeds a LazyMind insight** — A one-line AI observation differentiates the digest from "just another summary email." Generated server-side at send time.
- **Dynamic decision-digest gradient** — Blue→purple gradient header reinforces Decision DNA brand visually even in plaintext-stripped previews.
- **Single shared layout component** — `EmailLayout` wraps every template, ensuring header/footer/unsubscribe are uniform.
